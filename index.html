AI-Powered Support Triage Assistant

Problem

  • 30-50% of support tickets are "how-to" questions, not actual bugs
  • Support teams spend 30-40% of time manually categorizing and routing tickets
  • Delayed responses lead to customer frustration and churn
  • Misrouted tickets create bottlenecks and increase resolution time

Approach

  • Analyzed 6 months of support ticket data to identify patterns and categories
  • Designed ML-based classification system using NLP to auto-categorize tickets
  • Built auto-routing logic based on ticket type, urgency, and team expertise
  • Implemented confidence scoring to flag ambiguous cases for human review
  • Created feedback loop to continuously improve model accuracy

Outcome

  • 40% reduction in manual ticket routing time
  • 30% improvement in routing accuracy
  • $150K-$250K estimated annual savings in support costs
  • 25% faster first-response times for customers
  • Support team can focus on complex issues requiring human expertise

Improving Digital Banking Onboarding & KYC

Problem

  • 45% of users abandon onboarding before completing account verification
  • 12-step process takes average of 14 minutes to complete
  • Poor error messaging leaves users confused about what went wrong
  • No save & resume functionality ‚Äî users must restart if interrupted
  • 24-48 hour document verification delays cause anxiety and drop-offs

Approach

  • Mapped current 12-step journey and identified 5 major friction points
  • Redesigned flow to 7 streamlined steps using progressive disclosure
  • Integrated real-time ID verification (OCR + liveness detection)
  • Implemented smart error messaging with actionable guidance
  • Added save & resume functionality with progress indicators
  • Used plain language and explained why information was needed

Outcome

  • Drop-off reduced from 45% to 18% (60% improvement)
  • Time-to-completion decreased from 14 min to 5 min (64% reduction)
  • Completion rate improved from 55% to 82% (+27 points)
  • Support tickets during onboarding reduced by 40%
  • $4.05M incremental revenue from 135K additional completed accounts annually

Reducing Support Ticket Volume for B2B Project Management SaaS

Problem

  • 42% of support tickets are "how do I..." questions
  • 18% are feature location or workflow questions
  • Only 20% are actual bugs requiring engineering investigation
  • Critical issues get buried under repetitive "how-to" questions
  • New users struggle with onboarding, leading to early churn

Approach

  • Analyzed 6 months of ticket data to identify top question categories
  • Designed 4-part in-product guidance system:
    • Contextual help panels triggered by user behavior
    • Interactive walkthroughs for complex workflows
    • Template library with smart recommendations
    • Intelligent FAQ with AI-powered search
  • Used RICE framework to prioritize features for MVP vs. Phase 2
  • Defined success metrics across support efficiency and user experience

Outcome

  • 40-50% reduction in "how-to" support tickets projected
  • 30% improvement in feature adoption rates expected
  • Time-to-first-value reduced from 14 days to 5 days
  • $150K-$250K annual savings in support costs (10-person team)
  • Support team capacity freed to focus on proactive improvements
